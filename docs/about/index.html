About US

We are a Singapore based Fintech startup with subsidiaries in the US and India, using artificial intelligence (AI) to deliver Conversational Banking services. We help banks and credit unions create intelligent virtual assistants, bringing in automation and insightful customer engagement. The company’s conversational banking technology uses advanced NLP and machine intelligence to enable customers to have natural dialogues over messaging, voice or IoT devices.
